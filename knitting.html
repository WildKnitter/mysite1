Knitting is the New Yoga--NOT

There is a common misconception that my favorite hobby knitting is The New Yoga.

Anyone who has ever struggled with the kitchner stitch, has pounded their head against a wall attempting to modify a pattern that clearly was written by someone who can't add 2 + 2, or has stayed up all Christmas Eve to feverishly finish a major present that JUST ISN'T DONE YET--would beg to differ.
